Photo Cart

Photo Cart is a web application that focuses on adding photos to a cart and placing orders. it aims to provide users with a seamless and intuitive experience for browsing and selecting photos for purchase.

Live Link

App Overview

The header section contains a cart icon and a clickable text. Clicking the text displays all the available photos in the app. When the clickable text is clicked, all the available photos are displayed. Each photo is accompanied by a heart icon and a add icon. Clicking the add icon indicates that the user has selected the photo. The cart section is located on the other end of the page. Clicking the cart icon opens up the cart display. The cart display shows all the selected photos along with their respective prices. As more photos are added, the total price updates accordingly. In the cart display, users have the option to remove photos. Each selected photo is accompanied by a delete button to remove it from the cart. The price is automatically updated when a photo is added or removed from the cart. The "Place Order" button is available in the cart display. Clicking this button allows users to complete their order.

This cart capstone project offers users a way to browse and select photos, view the selected photos with their prices in the cart, and complete the order. This app provides a convenient and user-friendly experience for users to select and purchase their desired photos.
