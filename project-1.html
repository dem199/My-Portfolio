Food Ordering App

This is a food ordering app designed to satisfy your cravings with just a few taps. Whether you're seeking a quick bite or planning a lavish feast, it makes it effortless to explore a world of culinary delights and have them delivered right to your doorstep.

Live Link

App Overview

Upon loading the app, a modal pops up after a few seconds. The modal contains a form to collect user details, specifically the user's name and phone number. If the user fails to fill out the form, the modal prevents them from using the app further. The close button of the modal is not clickable until user input details. Additionally, the home page includes a section to add and delete food items from the DOM (Document Object Model). The Order page displays a variety of food options along with their prices and ingredients. Users can select multiple food items, and the app calculates the total price based on the items chosen. Users can also remove food items if they change their minds. After completing the order, a modal pops up requesting the user's card details for payment. Upon clicking the "Pay" button, a message is displayed to the user, informing them that the order is on its way. The message also includes the user's name. The About Us page provides details about the restaurant, such as its history, mission, and values. It also includes information about the chef, including their bio and a picture.

Regal cravings food ordering app offers users a seamless experience to order food. It starts with collecting user details, allows them to browse and select food items, calculates the total price, and handles the payment process. The app also provides information about the restaurant and the chef on the About Us page. The app can handle the necessary steps to finalize the order, such as entering payment information or providing contact details
